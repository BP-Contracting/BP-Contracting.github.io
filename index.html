Who we are

Arizona-born builders, built on experience.

At Build Play Contracting, we are Arizona-born builders with a passion for creating safe, functional, and lasting outdoor spaces. Specializing in playground installations, shade structures, site furnishings, and surfacing systems, we bring experience, craftsmanship, and attention to detail to every project we undertake.

Our team combines more than 10 years of construction industry experience, giving us the knowledge and expertise to successfully manage projects of all sizes. As Arizona locals, we understand the unique challenges of building in our desert environment and take pride in helping schools, municipalities, parks, HOAs, and communities create spaces where people can gather, play, and connect.

What truly sets us apart is our hands-on approach. We are actively involved in every project from start to finish, ensuring quality workmanship, clear communication, and a commitment to exceeding expectations. Our team is CPSI (Certified Playground Safety Inspector) certified, providing an added level of expertise and dedication to safety on every playground we build and maintain.

We don't just build projects—we build relationships. Our team is driven by professionalism, integrity, and a genuine passion for what we do. Whether we're installing a new playground, constructing a shade structure, or revitalizing an outdoor space, our goal remains the same: delivering exceptional results that serve Arizona communities for years to come.
