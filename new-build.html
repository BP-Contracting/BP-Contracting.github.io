What we do

Everything your new build needs.

From equipment installation and shade structures to site furnishings and civil work — we handle everything it takes to deliver a finished, ready-to-use outdoor space.

Amenities

Playground equipment

Professional installation of commercial play structures built to national safety standards and ADA requirements.

Shade structures

Steel ramadas and fabric shade sails installed to keep your space comfortable and functional year-round.

Site furnishings

Picnic tables, benches, BBQs, bike racks, waste receptacles, bleachers — everything that makes a site complete.

Surfacing

Poured-in-place rubber

Installation of PIP rubber surfacing that meets fall height requirements and holds up to heavy use over time.

Engineered wood fiber

ADA-accessible engineered wood fiber (EWF) surfacing — a cost-effective option that meets fall-height safety requirements.

Turf

Synthetic turf surfacing for play areas and recreation spaces — durable, low-maintenance, and clean year-round.

Repairs & Replacements

Equipment repairs

We fix damaged or worn play equipment — from broken components to structural repairs — so your playground stays safe and in service.

Equipment replacement

Ready to swap out old or outdated pieces? We remove existing equipment and install new units cleanly and efficiently.

Civil & Site Work

Sidewalk & curbing

New sidewalks, curbing, access points, and seat walls installed to code and built to last.

Excavation & grading

Site grading, excavation, and compacted sub-base work to set your project up with a solid foundation.

Concrete slabs

Flatwork and equipment pads poured to spec — clean, level, and ready for whatever goes on top.
