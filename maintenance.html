FAQ

Common questions.

How often should a playground be inspected? +

CPSC guidelines recommend a comprehensive safety inspection at least once a year. High-traffic facilities benefit from bi-annual inspections. Routine maintenance visits also include a walkthrough for any visible hazards.

What does a CPSI inspection include? +

A full inspection covers equipment condition, hardware integrity, surfacing depth and impact attenuation, ADA accessibility, age-appropriate design, and entrapment hazards. You receive a comprehensive photo report with an itemized list of any recommended repairs or replacements.

What surfacing types do you work with? +

We maintain and replenish poured-in-place rubber, wood fiber chips, sand, and rubber nuggets. We also offer full surfacing conversions if you want to switch from one material to another.

Do you work with HOAs and municipalities? +

Yes — we regularly service HOA communities, city parks, schools, and apartment complexes across Arizona. We can set up recurring maintenance schedules or provide one-time services as needed.

Is an inspection included with maintenance? +

Yes. Every scheduled maintenance visit includes a safety walkthrough at no extra charge. If a more thorough CPSI-certified inspection is needed, we can add that to any visit.
